7. Now comes the night of Enitharmons joy. Who shall I call? Who shall I send? That Woman, lovely Woman! may have dominion? Arise O Rintrah thee I call! & Palamabron thee! Go! tell the human race that Womans love is Sin!    That an Eternal life awaits the worms of sixty winters In an allegorical abode where existence hath never come; Forbid all Joy, & from her childhood shall the little female Spread nets in every secret path.

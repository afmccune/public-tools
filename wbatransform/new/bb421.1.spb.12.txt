10 But he knoweth the way that I take when he hath tried me I shall come forth like gold Have pity upon me! Have pity upon me! O ye my friends for the hand of God hath touched me Though he slay me yet will I trust in him The Just Upright Man is laughed to scorn Man that is born of a Woman is of few days & full of trouble he cometh up like a flower & is cut down. he fleeth also as a shadow & continueth not, And dost thou open thine eyes upon such a one & bringest me into judgment with thee WBlake invenit & sculp London. Published as the Act directs March 8: 1825. by William Blake N 3 Fountain Court Strand Proof

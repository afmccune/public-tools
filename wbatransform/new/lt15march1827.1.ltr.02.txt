Dear Sir This is to thank you                         for Two Pounds now by me recievd on account I have recievd a Letter from Mr Cumberland in which he says he will take                         one Copy of Job for himself but cannot as yet find a Customer                         for one but hopes to do somewhat by perseverance in his                         Endeavours he tells me that it is too much Finishd or over Labourd for his Bristol Friends as they think I saw Mr Tatham Senr yesterday he sat with me above an hour & lookd over the Dante he expressd himself very much pleasd with the design as well as the Engravings I am getting on with the Engravings                         & hope soon to get Proof. of what I am doing I am dear Sir Yours Sincerely 15 March 1827William Blake

19 The Lord maketh  Poor & maketh Rich He bringeth Low & Lifteth Up who provideth for the Raven his Food When his young ones cry unto God Every one also gave him a piece of Money Who remembered us in our low estate For his Mercy endureth for ever WBlake inv & sculp London. Published as the Act directs March 8: 1825, by William Blake N 3 Fountain Court Strand Proof

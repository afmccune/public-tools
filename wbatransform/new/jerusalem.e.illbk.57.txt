But Albion fled from the Divine Vision. with the Plow  of Nations enflaming The Living Creatures maddend and Albion fell into the  Furrow, and The Plow went over him & the Living was Plowed in  among the Dead But his Spectre rose over the starry Plow. Albion fled  beneath the Plow Till he came to the Rock of Ages. & he took his Seat  upon the Rock.

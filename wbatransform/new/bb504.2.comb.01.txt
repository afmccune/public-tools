Blake, del. et Sculp.
Theocritus,
A Grecian Poet, of Sicily Flourished 273 years
before the Birth of Christ, and quitting his
native country, governed by Hiero, King of
Syracuse, went over to King Ptolemy in Egypt,
by whom he was greatly patronized.
Vide Idyllium, 17.
London, Published by Dr. Thornton, 1821.

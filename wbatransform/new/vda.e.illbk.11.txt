The sea fowl takes the wintry blast. for a cov'ring to her limbs: And the wild snake, the pestilence to adorn him with gems & gold. And trees. & birds. & beasts, & men. behold their eternal joy. Arise you little glancing wings, and sing your infant joy! Arise and drink your bliss. for every thing that lives is holy! Thus every morning wails Oothoon. but Theotormon sits Upon the margind ocean conversing with shadows dire, The Daughters of Albion hear her woes. & eccho back her sighs. The End

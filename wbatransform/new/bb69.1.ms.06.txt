6There let Hymen oft appear In Saffron Robe with Taper clear With Mask & antique Pageantry Such Sights as Youthful Poets dream On Summers Eve by haunted Stream Then to the well trod Stage anon If Johnsons learned Sock be on Or Sweetest Shakespeare Fancys Child Warble his native wood notes wild The youthful Poet sleeping on a bank by the Haunted Stream by Sun Set sees in his dream the more bright Sun of Sun of Imagination. under the auspices of Shakespeare & Johnson. in which is Hymen at a Marriage & the Antique Pageantry attending it

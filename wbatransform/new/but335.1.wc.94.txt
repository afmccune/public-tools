2 Ode for Music. 1. Fame. 2. A bird singing 3. A Genius driving away "Comus & his midnight crew" 4.Milton struck the corded Shell Newtons self bends from his State Sublime 5. "I wood the gleam of Cynthia silver bright Where willowy Camus lingers with delight" 6. "Great Edward with the lillies on his brow To hail the festal morning come" 7. "Leaning from her golden cloud The venerable Margaret" 8. "The Laureate wreathe" 9. "Nor fear the rocks nor seek the shore" 10. Fame.

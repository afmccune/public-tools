Epicurus.
A Grecian Philosopher, Flourished 264 years before
Christ, His philosophy was transfused into Latin,
by the great Roman Poet Lucretius, who
flourished 105 years before Christ, and wrote
his "De Rerum Naturâ" on the Nature of
Things. He placed the summum bonum on
Tranquillity of Mind, arising from Virtue, and
The Contemplation of Nature.
He is said to have been born 342 B.C. Died 270 B.C. æ: 72.
London, Published by Dr. Thornton, 1821.

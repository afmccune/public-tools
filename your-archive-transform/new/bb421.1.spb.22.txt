20
How precious are thy thoughts
unto me O God
how great is the sum of them
There were not found, Women fair as the Daughters of Job
in all the Land & their Father gave them Inheritance
among their Brethren
If I ascend up into Heaven thou art there
If I make my bed in Hell behold Thou
art there
WBlake invenit & sc
London. Published as the Act directs March 8: 1825 by William Blake No3 Fountain Court Strand
